## 3. Images

### Image Specifications

| Type | Dimensions | Format | Notes |
|------|------------|--------|-------|
| **Experience Card** | 1200 x 800 px (3:2 ratio) | JPG or WebP | Landscape orientation, high quality |
| **Hero Background** | 1920 x 1080 px (16:9 ratio) | JPG or WebP | For homepage hero section |

### Image Requirements

- **Quality**: High resolution, not pixelated when displayed
- **Orientation**: Landscape (horizontal) only
- **Content**: No text overlays, no watermarks, no logos
- **Style**: Real photos preferred over stock (authentic Kenya feel)
- **Lighting**: Well-lit, vibrant colors
- **Subject**: Clear focal point (animal, beach, mountain, etc.)

## Questions?

If anything is unclear, ask before filling out. It's better to clarify upfront than to redo data entry.

Common questions:
- **"What price should I use?"** → Use your standard season, mid-range accommodation price as the "From". Use peak season or slightly upgraded as the "To".
- **"How many inclusions?"** → 4-6 items. Focus on the valuable stuff (park fees, meals, transport) not obvious things.
- **"What if I don't have an image?"** → Note it in the checklist. We can use placeholders temporarily or I can suggest stock sources.
- **"Can I add more experiences later?"** → Yes, the system will support adding more. Start with 10-15 strong ones.
